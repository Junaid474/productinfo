Overview

If you are tired of shiny, oily ABS keycaps, this PBT set is a massive upgrade. The MDA profile offers a unique ergonomic height that is lower than SA but higher than Cherry, making it fantastic for typing accuracy. The legends are printed using Thermal Sublimation, meaning the Japanese and English characters will never fade away.
